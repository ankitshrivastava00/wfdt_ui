SCOPE OF WORK

Experts at the World Fund for Development and Planning design and implement a methodology of development trends and practical measures which constitute necessary approaches for expansion of studies and researches, aiding in scientific, economic, social and environmental development for individuals and institutions alike. Sustainable scientific partnerships and investment in communities provides a brighter future when environmental and human factors, as well as natural resources that are necessary for continued production of economic factors are taken into consideration.

Programs

PROGRAM SECTOR

The Program Sector of WFDP seeks to create awareness and promote development of science and practical knowledge to process positive change and realistic results from the target audience interested in this regard, to adopt those ideas and trends posed by the Fund’s programs.

The Program Sector works in collaboration with the Activity Sector and Enterprise Sector to achieve tactical objectives, which are within the framework of what has been approved by the senior management of the Fund, in coordination with all branches, across the world.

The Program Sector focuses on:

  • Health Enhancement
  • Environmental Development
  • Social Development
  • Educational Development
  • Economic Development
  • Agricultural Development

As part of the 2018-2019 action plan, the President of the Fund approved a Memorandum of Understanding, for establishing new initiatives namely

  • The Continental Forum for Co-Economics” (International Development Program)
  • Women’s Life Energy” Festival (International Program and Event).
  • Humanitarian Knights” award program for effective humanitarian service (International Scientific and Development Program).
  • The annual “Conference for Exchange and Development of Ideas, Talents, Expertise and Experience” (International Cultural and Professional Program and Event).
  • International License for Management and Investment” (Diploma Certificate Program).
  • Global Program for Scientific and Practical Development of Civil Society” (International Program and Certification).
