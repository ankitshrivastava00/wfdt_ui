OUR HUMANITARIAN MISSION

WE BELIEVE. Therefore we continuously strive to pave the way for future generations. We aim to construct the utopian ideal of a civilization of the finest structure. One that honors and implements fair economic and cultural best practices and of course, a transparent media. Where communities do not merely co-exist, but live harmoniously whilst reaping the benefits of a society that cultivates responsible freedom, equal rights and promotes freedom of thought and speech.

Women Empowerment

Women are The Driving Force of Families and Communities:

Globally there are more women in power today, than ever before. Women are leaders in economy, science, politics, industry and technology. WFDP recognizes the importance of paving the way for more women to participate across all sectors, as this is the key to building a strong economy in communities that need a driving economic force. One of the major International Affairs agendas is improving the quality of life for women, as this in turn improves the quality of life for children, men, families and communities as a whole

WFDP’s principles of empowering women has been developed in conjunction with the United Nations Women’s Global Charter for Humanity and is available as a practical guideline on how to empower women in the workplace, marketplace and community, both in the private and public sector. The principles have been designed to support existing companies in reviewing current policies and practices and to the benefit of new institutions as well.

In short the principles are:

  • Advocate gender equality by encouraging and supporting women to lead top companies
  • Ensure equal respect, non-discrimination and fair treatment of all employees at work (men and women)
  • Guarantee health, safety and stability of all employees
  • Improve the efficiency of education, training and professional development for women
  • Support equal rights initiatives throughout the community
