SCOPE OF WORK

Experts at the World Fund for Development and Planning design and implement a methodology of development trends and practical measures which constitute necessary approaches for expansion of studies and researches, aiding in scientific, economic, social and environmental development for individuals and institutions alike. Sustainable scientific partnerships and investment in communities provides a brighter future when environmental and human factors, as well as natural resources that are necessary for continued production of economic factors are taken into consideration.

Activities

The Activities Sector of WFDP is one of the most important operational sectors involving the interests of the industry. The President and the team at the Fund believe that lectures, seminars, conferences and workshops are the most important means of dissemination of ideas, experiences and insights, as well as production and presentation of researches and studies. WFDP encourages and organizes positive interaction with civil society and specialized communities in industry, agriculture, women empowerment, enhancing investment performance and administrative cadres in charge of institutions dealing with development and development administration.

Worldwide, outstanding evidence of the Fund’s mission, has taken shape in the form of research and study centers, expedited and established by WFDP. Lecture and seminar workshops around the world, in addition to numerous successful visits to primary schools, elderly care centers and centers for special needs, led by the President of the Fund. These visits have been fundamental in assessing the living conditions, level of education and welfare of the inhabitants of the disadvantaged segment of the society and for creating a tailor-made solution to support their needs.

Activities include:

  • Conferences
  • Forums
  • Media Workshops
  • Cultural Exchange Center and Fairs
  • Caravans
  • Contests and Festivals
